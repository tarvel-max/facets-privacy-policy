# Privacy Policy for Facets App

Last updated: April 8, 2026

## Introduction
Facets ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Facets mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app.

## Information We Collect
- **Camera**: We access your camera to capture facial and palm images for analysis purposes.
- **Images**: Photos taken within the app are temporarily processed for AI analysis and are not stored on our servers.
- **Date of Birth**: Used for Saju (Four Pillars) analysis only.

## How We Use Your Information
- To provide AI-based analysis (face reading, palm reading, Saju, MBTI)
- Images and data are sent to AI services (Anthropic Claude, Upstage Solar) for analysis
- We do not permanently store your personal images or analysis results

## Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party AI services:
- Anthropic Claude API (https://www.anthropic.com)
- Upstage Solar API (https://www.upstage.ai)

## Data Security
We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data during transmission.

## Children's Privacy
Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age.
